What Is A Special Inspection?

A plain-language overview of why special inspections matter during structural construction.

Special inspections are focused checks performed during construction to confirm that certain work is completed in accordance with approved documents and code requirements.

Why They Matter

  • They support code compliance and quality control
  • They help document critical structural work
  • They give owners and project teams clearer visibility into field conditions

Because H&Z is a NYC DOB Registered Special Inspection Agency, the firm can support projects that require inspection coordination and documentation.
